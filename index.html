About

The Designer & Developer

Kathryn Kingsberry

"If you're trying to create a company, it's like baking a cake. You have to have all ingredients in the right proportion." - Elon Musk

Kathryn Kingsberry, based in Encino, California, developed a passion for web design and development after working for several years at a software company that specialized in web-based data management solutions. After traveling to a number of non-profits nationwide to conduct workshops for organizations using the software, she realized just how vital technology is in enriching individual lives and communities as a whole.

This realization inspired her to complete an intensive course in fullstack web devlopment at UCLA and begin building websites for inidividuals and businesses tirelessly working to improve the world around them. In this process, she has learned that it does indeed require the right ingredients for an organization to thrive and one of these ingredients is an effective online presence. Through Kingsberry Web Solutions, her goal is to assist budding entrepreneurs and a variety of organizations to build and maintain that presence.
